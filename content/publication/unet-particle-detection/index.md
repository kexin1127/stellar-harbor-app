abstract: >
  We present an Improved U-Net for high-density particle cluster center localization on 64×64 simulated Time Projection Chamber (TPC) images. We built a dataset of 10,000 Gaussian-based particle heatmaps and designed a full evaluation pipeline with Efficiency, Fake Rate, Bias, and Resolution metrics. The model improves localization accuracy through dilated-convolution bottlenecks, dropout regularization, and a hybrid MSE+BCE loss. Benchmarked against CoG, Original U-Net, U2-Net, and U-Net++, the proposed method shows stronger accuracy and robustness in dense overlapping scenarios.
