Supervisor: Prof. Gunther Roland, Massachusetts Institute of Technology

- Developed an Improved U-Net for high-density particle cluster center localization on 64×64 simulated TPC images.
- Built a dataset of 10,000 Gaussian-based particle heatmaps and designed a full evaluation pipeline with Efficiency, Fake Rate, Bias, and Resolution metrics.
- Improved localization accuracy through dilated-convolution bottlenecks, dropout regularization, and a hybrid MSE+BCE loss.
- Benchmarked the model against CoG, Original U-Net, U2-Net, and U-Net++, showing stronger accuracy and robustness in dense overlapping scenarios.
- Co-authored a paper accepted by TOCS 2025 as co-first author.
